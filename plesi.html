Plesi Android App

Developed as a part of the Hopkins User Interfaces and Mobile Applications Class, the Plesi App allows users to find new local businesses by:

  • Searching and filtering by business type, price range, minority owned businesses, and more.
  • Favoriting businesses and saving them to a list.
  • Sharing businesses with friends through quick action buttons

Tech Stack

  • Java for Android Development
  • Firebase Auth and Realtime database
