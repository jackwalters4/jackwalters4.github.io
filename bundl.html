BundL

BundL is a fintech startup focusing on the future of the loyalty and rewards space.

BundL is a centralized debit card program that allows merchants to push personalized, targeted loyalty rewards to our users.

Through our mobile app, users can sign up for a BundL card and immediately receive loyalty rewards on their transactions to their favorite restaurants and retail stores

Founded by my two friends and I, we've worked hard to get an iOS mobile app to beta release that currently has 25 users.
By partnering with card issuing company, Apto Payments, BundL users have:

  • Made over 335 Transactions with their BundL Card
  • Spent over $4,500
  • Earned over $205

The beta version of BundL provides cash back where users shop the most
